Compactness


Local Open Scope topology_scope.

Section compact.
  Variable X : Type.
  Variable T : Topology X.

  Definition compact := ∀ Xj : Cover X, cover_open T Xj
                                             → ∃ Xj' : Cover X, Finite _ Xj' ∧ subcover Xj' Xj. End compact.
