Our research:

The following joint research Puneet Batra and Bogdan Dobrescu was undertaken while I was a graduate student at UC Berkeley.

Anomaly-Free Sets of Fermions. The goal given to me was to find integer solutions to the system
x1 + x2 + ... + xn = 0,
(x1)3 + (x2)3 + ... + (xn)3 = 0.

It was published in the Journal of Mathematical Physics, 2006.
